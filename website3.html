Attica Prison riot

Who lead the Riot?

The man who organized the riot was L.D. Barkley. He was considered a influential leader for the riot who got the people going. He was arrested for driving without his license and believed this was unfair and did not fit the crime. He kept claiming how he wanted to help the community and needed to leave the prison so he arranged the riot. His plan was for good to come out pg the riot. Good did come out of it however as there were positive changes that were made to the prison. L.D. Barkley

What was the medias reaction?

A large part of the riot was how the media reacted to it. The media had taken over this riot and gave it much attention. It was front cover of almost every news papers making every headline. People believed the media had ruined the riot as the media portrayed it as the prisoners were not the victims. This was very big information because it influenced how people felt about.

Did people get hurt?

There were many people that got hurt in the riot. There were many people that also got killed in the riot.
